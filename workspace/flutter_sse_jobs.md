## General Role & Salary Overview (from Hired)

A Senior Flutter SSE is expected to go beyond just writing code. The role is a blend of technical leadership and hands-on development.

#### **Salary**

According to Levels.fyi, compensation for software engineers at companies working with Flutter in the US can be quite competitive.

- **Total Compensation Range:** Approximately **$114K to $313K+** per year.
- **Median Compensation:** Around **$189K** per year.
- This package typically includes base salary, stock options, and bonuses.

_(Source: Levels.fyi)_
